Our Story

A Century of
Crafting Trust

In 1912, Keshavlal Kalidas Chokshi opened a humble goldsmith workshop in Padra, Gujarat. His vision was simple yet profound — to offer gold jewellery of uncompromising purity, with transparent pricing and personal care.

Over 112 years later, that vision endures. What began as one artisan's promise has grown into a heritage trusted by four generations of families across Baroda. Every piece we create still carries forward our founder's unwavering commitment to quality, honesty, and the belief that jewellery should be as genuine as the moments it celebrates.

Our Mission
